üá™üá∫ Euro Adoption in Bulgaria

From January 1, 2026, Bulgaria officially joins the Eurozone and the euro becomes the only legal tender. Key dates and rules:

August 8, 2025

Dual Price Display

All prices in stores, menus, and online must be displayed in both leva and euro.

January 1, 2026

Euro Becomes Official Currency

All bank accounts, salaries, and pensions are automatically converted to euro at the fixed rate.

January 1-31, 2026

Dual Circulation Period

Payments can be made in leva or euro, but change is returned only in euro. If no euro available - in leva.

February 1, 2026

Euro Only

All payments are made only in euro. The lev is withdrawn from circulation.

August 8, 2026

End of Dual Display

Prices are displayed only in euro.
