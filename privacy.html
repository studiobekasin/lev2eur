Lev2EUR is a free currency calculator for converting between Euro (EUR) and Bulgarian Lev (BGN), developed by Studio Bekasin.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect personal information. The app works entirely locally on your device. All calculations are performed offline and no data is sent to servers.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

The web version uses cookies to save your preferences (language, theme) and to personalize advertisements.

User Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in Google settings
  • Delete app data from device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time
