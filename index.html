üí± About Lev2EUR

This calculator helps workers and cashiers calculate change when customers pay with a mix of EUR and BGN currencies.

The exchange rate is fetched live from the European Central Bank via the Frankfurter API. Fallback rate: 1 EUR = 1.9558 BGN (fixed rate).

Use the "Split Change" section to calculate how much BGN to give back if you're giving part of the change in EUR.
